Making videos? What a rush.

Adobe Premiere Rush is the free mobile and desktop video editing app for creativity on the go. Wherever you are, from your phone to your computer, you can shoot, edit, and share high-quality videos. Fun, intuitive, and as fast as social media, it’s the easiest way to star in your followers’ feeds.

Download the Premiere Rush app for free on macOS, Windows, iOS, and Android.

Questions? We’ve got answers.

What is Adobe Premiere Rush?

Premiere Rush is the all-in-one way to create videos across devices. Capture and edit in 4K from your phone, or continue on your computer to complete your edits effortlessly.

Is Premiere Rush free?

Start using Premiere Rush for free with access to unlimited exports, easy editing and effects, fully customizable graphics, and a library of royalty-free sounds. Upgrade to the Premiere Rush Single App plan for US$9.99/mo to add unlimited exports, project syncing across mobile and desktop devices, and 100GB of cloud storage (with the ability to upgrade to 10TB). Premiere Rush is also included with Premiere Pro.
